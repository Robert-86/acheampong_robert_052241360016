Kumasi Technical University was established in 1954 as Kumasi Technical Institute (K. T. I. ) to offer craft courses. In 1963, the Institute was converted to a non-tertiary Polytechnic status under the Ghana Education Service to start offering, in addition, technician diploma and sub- professional courses. The Polytechnic Law, 1992 (PNDC L.321) elevated the Polytechnic to a tertiary institution to provide high calibre skilled manpower with reference to manufacturing, commerce, science and technology to act as a catalyst for technological development. As a Polytechnic it was one of the famous, elegant and vibrant Polytechnics in Ghana.

Our Mission, Vision & Core Value

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering top-notch education and research in engineering, science, technology, and entrepreneurship, fostering the growth of industries in Ghana. Our dedication lies in providing quality teaching that equips students to contribute effectively to the nation's industrial development, shaping a future where innovation and academic excellence drive sustainable progress.

Our Vision

Dedicated to excellence, our world-class technical university is a beacon for science, technology, and entrepreneurship education. We aspire to empower students to thrive in the dynamic landscape of innovation and global progress. Through cutting-edge research and visionary leadership, we shape graduates who catalyze positive change on a global scale.

Our Values

Integrity : We set and maintain the pinnacle of educational standards, earning the trust of stakeholders through unwavering commitment to excellence.
Trailblazers : We lead the way in pioneering innovations in scholarship and research, addressing both local and national challenges with a forward-thinking mindset.
Dedication : Our commitment is resolute as we strive to fulfill our mandate, meeting and exceeding the expectations of all stakeholders with unwavering dedication.
