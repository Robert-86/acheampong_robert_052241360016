Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science is under the Faculty of Applied Sciences and has been training students in Higher National Diploma programme since 2007. Currently the department runs HND in Computer Science, Diploma in Information Technology, Diploma in Hardware and Networking, Diploma in Web Application and Database Management, and other certificate courses.

Objectives

Committed to empowering Certificate, HND, and B.Tech students, we envision a transformative learning setting. Beyond traditional education, we pioneer 'Software Studios'—innovative spaces with preprogrammed components, revolutionizing software development in Ghana. Our commitment extends to reshaping the educational landscape through these cutting-edge environments. Join us in redefining the future of software development.

  • Set up modern computer systems for individuals and corporate bodies.
  • Manage existing computer and neural systems.
  • Develop various hardware components.
  • Provide comprehensive management services for sustained efficiency.
  • Explore and integrate the latest technological advancements.
  • Commitment to ongoing care and enhancement of computer systems
  • Focus on the development of innovative hardware components.
  • Holistic approach covering setup, management, and ongoing development.
  • Forward-looking approach to adapt to evolving technological needs.
  • Embrace the synergy of hardware and software for comprehensive solutions.
